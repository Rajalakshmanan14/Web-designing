3DTV Play

Following the posting of the final driver from Release 418 in April 2019, GeForce Game Ready Drivers will no longer
support NVIDIA 3D Vision. The NVIDIA support team will continue to address critical driver issues for 3D Vision in Release 418 through
April, 2020. Those looking to utilize 3D Vision can remain on a Release 418 driver.



Our software that enables the use of 3D gaming with 3D TVs, 3DTV Play, is now included for free in Release 418. It
is no longer available as a standalone download. Our 3D Vision Video Player will continue to be offered as a standalone download, for
free, until the end of 2019.
